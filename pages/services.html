SERVICES

Access to Medicines from Anywhere in the World

Supply of Licensed and Unlicensed Medicines

We understand that every clinical case is unique and that access to specific medicines can be critically important. That's why we offer flexible, personalized logistics solutions with a strong focus on safety, legality, and delivery efficiency.

What we offer:
• Supply of licensed medicines directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors.
• Sourcing and delivery of unlicensed medicines upon physician request (Named Patient Supply), in full compliance with international and local regulations.
• Full-cycle support — from inquiry and alternative selection to import, documentation, and shipment tracking.
• Cooperation only with reliable partners who comply with GDP standards and hold valid WDA licenses.
• Individual case management: each request is handled with attention to the needs of the specific patient or healthcare institution.

Supply of Medicines for Clinical Trials

We offer comprehensive solutions for the provision of clinical trial medicines — from sourcing and procurement of comparators to organizing GDP-compliant delivery.

We partner with CROs, pharmaceutical companies, and contract manufacturers to ensure full compliance with the requirements of each project.

What we offer:
• Supply of comparators and reference products with full documentation confirming legality and quality of origin.
• Ability to work with both international and local manufacturers, depending on study objectives.
• Personalized batch planning: selection of specific quantities, batches, dosage forms, and expiry dates.
• Certification, labeling, and packaging in accordance with study protocols and sponsor requirements.
• Reliable logistics and distribution per GDP standards, including temperature control, shipment tracking, and documentation flow.
• Project support by an experienced team, considering all aspects of clinical protocols and regulatory compliance.

Global Wholesale Trade

We provide access to international medicines for patients and organizations around the world. Thanks to our flexible approach, broad partner network, and expert support, we deliver high-quality pharmaceutical solutions in line with local regulations in each country.

Our team:
• Works efficiently with a global network of trusted manufacturers and suppliers;
• Selects optimal options in terms of delivery time, conditions, and pricing;
• Coordinates the supply process from product search to final delivery.

Logistics, Warehousing, and Distribution

We manage the full logistics cycle of pharmaceutical products — from receiving and storage to distribution to pharmacies, clinics, and hospitals. We ensure reliable delivery of both licensed and unlicensed medicines, fully compliant with regulatory requirements and GDP standards.

Our team oversees every step — from documentation handling to maintaining storage conditions and temperature compliance. We understand the importance of time and quality, especially when dealing with medicines for specific patients or limited batches.

We provide:
• Storage in certified facilities with temperature and humidity control systems;
• Preparation of all necessary documentation (including import licenses, quality and origin certificates, etc.);
• Organization of logistics within Russia and internationally, with tracking and timeline control;
• A personalized approach: we work with both large suppliers and private pharmacy requests;
• Transparency and accountability at every stage — from the first inquiry to final delivery to the end user.
