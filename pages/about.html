About us

We believe that every person deserves a full life. And we do everything to make that possible.

Axivita Pharm
Your trusted partner in the supply of rare, prescription, and hard-to-access medicines.

We are an international pharmaceutical company specializing in the search, procurement, and delivery of unlicensed and scarce medicinal products.
We help patients and medical organizations gain access to essential medications, even if they are unavailable from local suppliers.

Mission

Making the unavailable accessible by connecting patients with life-saving medicines in the shortest possible time — through technology, care, and efficient logistics.

Core principles

Stability, quality, and promptness.

By working directly with verified manufacturers, license holders, and certified distributors worldwide, we ensure reliable pharmaceutical logistics.

The Axivita Pharm team possesses deep expertise in international regulation, allowing us to effectively handle even the most complex challenges in medicine supply.

Values

Our values reflect the company’s philosophy, shape our approach to partnerships, interactions with patients and society, and define the direction of our development.

Accessibility

The company strives to make medications for complex and rare diseases available to every patient, regardless of their location or financial situation.

Partnership

Openness to cooperation with the medical community, charitable foundations, and other interested parties.

Caring for People

The company does more than supply medicines — it works to improve people’s quality of life by offering support, consultations, and a personalized approach to each case.

Innovation

The company utilizes modern technologies in logistics, analytics, and supply chain management to ensure the fast and efficient delivery of life-saving medicines.

Responsibility

The company understands that lives depend on its work, which is why it adheres to the highest standards of quality, safety, and ethics in the supply of medical products.

Reliability

The company maintains open and honest communication with all clients and partners, guaranteeing fair business practices and accurate information about deliveries.

Our Advantages

Global Reach

We work with trusted suppliers and logistics partners worldwide, enabling us to quickly source and deliver essential medications, regardless of the country.

Personalized Approach

We tailor optimal procurement and logistics solutions based on the urgency and specifics of each order.

Speed and Accuracy

Minimal delivery times, tracking at every stage, and precise adherence to deadlines.

Compliance with Standards

Legal clarity and transparency. Strict compliance with international and local pharmaceutical regulations.

Quality and Safety

Verification of medication authenticity, transport, and storage conditions.

Flexibility and Support

Full transaction support and problem-solving at every stage.
