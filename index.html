About us

Reliable support where it matters most

We help patients, healthcare providers, and organizations find essential solutions when standard pathways are unavailable.

By working with certified manufacturers, licensed suppliers, and specialized distributors worldwide, we ensure the delivery of pharmaceuticals strictly in accordance with current regulations and international standards.

Our team consists of experts with deep knowledge of the pharmaceutical market, always ready to offer a tailored approach. We handle each request with care and strive to provide not just delivery — but trust and stability for everyone involved.

Get in touch with us to discuss how we can help you receive the right medicines at the right time.

Services

Supply of Licensed and Unlicensed Medicines

Access to both locally registered and hard-to-find or temporarily unavailable (unlicensed) medicines

Logistics, warehousing and distribution

We ensure reliable and fast delivery, storage in certified warehouses with temperature and humidity control, as well as full documentation transparency at every stage.

Supply of medicines for clinical trials

Comprehensive solutions for clinical trial supply — from sourcing and procurement of comparators to organizing GDP-compliant delivery.

Global wholesale distribution

An established network of suppliers, quality control, and full support throughout every stage of logistics. We provide flexible and secure solutions for the import and export of pharmaceutical products.

Quality without compromise

At AxiVita Pharm, we adhere to strict standards at every stage of our operations — from partner selection to the delivery of medicines. We work exclusively with trusted suppliers and logistics operators to ensure the safety and compliance of the entire supply chain.

All company processes comply with Good Distribution Practice (GDP) requirements, and our warehouse facilities are licensed according to current European regulations.
